AnalySAR — About

Built for the 2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge (Through the Radar Looking Glass). A web tool to explore flood dynamics in Tabasco, Mexico using Sentinel-1 SAR.

Stack: GEE · Mapbox GL · Turf.js · Chart.js Data: Sentinel-1 VV (IW) Exports: GeoJSON + CSV
